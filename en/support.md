## Get Support

Need help with LogicNodes? We're here to assist you.

### Contact Support

For technical support, account issues, or general inquiries, please contact our support team:

**Email:** [support@logicnodes.ai](mailto:support@logicnodes.ai)

**Response Time:** We aim to respond to all support requests within 24-48 hours during business days.

### What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us assist you more efficiently, please include:

- A clear description of your issue or question
- Any relevant error messages
- Steps to reproduce the problem (if applicable)
- Your account email address (if account-related)

### Privacy and Legal Inquiries

For privacy-related questions or legal matters, please contact:

**Email:** [legal@logicnodes.ai](mailto:legal@logicnodes.ai)
